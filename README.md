# The-Israeli-Industry-Job-Map
Link: https://www.industry.org.il/industry-job-map

*Overview*
An interactive, map-based platform that visualizes active job openings across the Israeli industry. Built as a custom data layer over Google Maps, it provides candidates with an intuitive, geographic overview of hiring companies, complete with company insights and direct HR contact details.

*My Role & Responsibilities*
Product Initiative: 
Conceptualized and deployed the platform to provide localized value beyond traditional job boards.

Data Management: 
Continuously aggregated, updated, and verified job postings from various industry companies to ensure a high-quality, real-time data flow.

Growth & Partnerships: 
Drove platform adoption to over 54,000 views through strategic cross-promotion. Leveraged ongoing partnerships with government ministries, the Israeli Employment Service, and NGOs, and aggressively marketed the map at employment fairs that I produced and hosted.

*Key Features*
Geospatial visualization of job openings using a Google Maps data layer.
Direct access to company profiles and CV submission emails.
Real-time data synchronization of open positions across the industrial sector.
